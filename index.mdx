## What is DeepVista?

DeepVista turns your instructions into **shared memory** that your team and AI agents follow automatically. Drop a simple note — DeepVista instantly generates a personalized playbook from it, then runs the entire workflow for you.

<Info>
  DeepVista is built on **VistaBase™**, a proprietary engine that converts your feedback into one persistent, shared source of truth across your entire execution stack.
</Info>

## Core concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="VistaBooks" icon="book">
    A **VistaBook** is a reusable AI playbook generated from a single note you write. Each VistaBook captures your intent, breaks it into steps, and executes the full workflow automatically — no manual configuration needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="VistaBase™" icon="database">
    **VistaBase™** is the shared memory layer that powers DeepVista. It stores every instruction, preference, and piece of feedback you give — making it available to your team members and AI agents in real time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Context cards" icon="id-card">
    **Context cards** are structured knowledge records inside your DeepVista project. They hold details about people, topics, and processes, and stay in sync across every tool you connect.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What am I actually connecting?

Some workflows ask you to set up **Claude Code**. Here's what that means, in plain terms:

- **Claude Code** runs on your own computer, and signs in with your own Claude account — not a DeepVista one. Your password never passes through our servers.
- On **claude.ai**, you turn on a setting that lets Claude read a tool you've connected, like Google Drive. That's the only place this kind of access gets granted.
- From there, DeepVista works through the Claude Code on your computer to fetch what it needs and bring the results back to you.

<Note>
  This uses your own Claude subscription's usage allowance, not a DeepVista quota — worth knowing if you're on a limited plan.
</Note>
