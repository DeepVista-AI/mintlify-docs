## What is DeepVista?

DeepVista turns your instructions into **shared memory** that your team and AI agents follow automatically. Drop a simple note — DeepVista instantly generates a personalized playbook from it, then runs the entire workflow for you.

It is designed for founders who spend hours repeating context to their team, their inbox, and their AI tools. With DeepVista, you say it once. Everyone — and every agent — gets it right the first time.

<Info>
  DeepVista is built on **VistaBase™**, a proprietary engine that converts your feedback into one persistent, shared source of truth across your entire execution stack.
</Info>

## Core concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="VistaBooks" icon="book">
    A **VistaBook** is a reusable AI playbook generated from a single note you write. Each VistaBook captures your intent, breaks it into steps, and executes the full workflow automatically — no manual configuration needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="VistaBase™" icon="database">
    **VistaBase™** is the shared memory layer that powers DeepVista. It stores every instruction, preference, and piece of feedback you give — making it available to your team members and AI agents in real time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Context cards" icon="id-card">
    **Context cards** are structured knowledge records inside your DeepVista project. They hold details about people, topics, and processes — and can be read, created, updated, or deleted directly from any connected AI client via the MCP server.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drop a note">
    Write a short instruction, feedback, or idea — just as you naturally would in a message or a note app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="DeepVista generates your playbook">
    DeepVista instantly turns your note into a structured VistaBook: a step-by-step playbook personalized to how you work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execution runs automatically">
    Your VistaBook executes the full workflow — no clicking, no micromanaging. Your team and agents follow the same playbook, every time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
