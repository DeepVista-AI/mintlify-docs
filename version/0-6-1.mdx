Here is a focused look at our recent feature updates for DeepVista v0.6.1. We’ve streamlined the interface and expanded our AI capabilities to give you more control!

## 🔥 Highlighted Features

<br />

### 🤖 Sub-Workflows

- **What’s New:** Workflows can now be broken down into reusable sub-workflows, making it easier to organize and scale complex processes.
- **User Value:** Users can progressively drill into high-level workflows, break them into concrete steps, and develop each part independently. This makes it easier to design, iterate on, and maintain sophisticated workflows without losing structure.

<video src="/videos/1.mp4" controls />

---

### 💬 Global Chat Component Refactor

- **What’s New:** The chat component has been completely overhauled and hoisted up to the home route.
- **User Value:** Previously, the chat was constrained to the context guard view and you wouldn't be able to see it if it was closed. It is now globally accessible across all fronts—including the home route, workflows, and nodes—while preserving its state.

---

### 🗺️ New Guided Onboarding Tour

- **What’s New:** We've introduced a new guided tour tutorial for new users.
- **User Value:** This walkthrough guides you cleanly through the core skill flow, ensuring you can discover and execute your first workflow with ease.
