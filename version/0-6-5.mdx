### **🔒 Seamless Collaboration with Context Card Locking**

When multiple team members are working together, keeping data safe is essential.

- **Edit Locking:** We have added a "lock" system for context cards. If you open a card that someone else is currently editing, a banner will notify you that the card is locked.
- **Take Over Feature:**  If you need to make changes immediately, you can choose to "Take Over" editing, which instantly updates the banner on the other user's screen. This works across all workflows, nodes, and context cards.

- **Advanced Filtering:** We've introduced robust multi-filter support for tables. You can now stack multiple filters on your datasets to find specific users or records in seconds.

### **💻 Turn Your Local Machine into a DeepVista Agent**

We have unlocked a powerful new way to execute tasks directly on your local hardware or remote cloud servers using the brand-new DeepVista CLI.

- **Local Task Delegation:** By running a simple background command on your machine (Mac, Windows, or Linux), your hardware will poll our server's task queue.
- **Run Complex Workflows Remotely:**  You can prompt the DeepVista agent from your phone while on the go, and it will securely dispatch and execute complex scripts or processes (like posting directly to social media) on your active machine back home.
