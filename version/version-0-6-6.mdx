Welcome to this week's DeepVista release update! This week, we shipped a series of upgrades designed to give you smarter workspace organization, fairer team credit sharing, and dynamic AI model routing that just works.

---

## What We Shipped This Week

### 🗂️ Smarter Workspace Context & Filters

- **Filter by Attributes:** Quickly narrow down notes, workflows, or context cards in VistaBase using targeted metadata attributes.

### 💳 Fairer Credit Allocation

- **Project-Level Credit Usage:** Workflow executions now deduct credits directly from the **project owner’s balance**, rather than penalizing individual team members who trigger the run.

### 🤖 Intelligent Model Routing & Generation Metrics

- **Automatic Model Selection:** DeepVista now dynamically routes your prompts to the optimal model. Simple chat queries automatically hit lightweight, ultra-fast models like **GLM** or **Kimi**, while heavy reasoning and task-oriented jobs route straight to powerhouses like **Claude**.
