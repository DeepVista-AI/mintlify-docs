June 23, 2026

We’ve rolled out a set of user experience enhancements and automation tools this week to help you manage your workflows and data with total precision. From grouping your entire workspace around specific projects to fully automated browser assistants, here is a look at what we shipped.

---

### **📂 A Smarter, Project-Scoped Workspace**

We have fundamentally rearranged how data is organized in DeepVista to make context switching seamless.

- **Project-Scoped Architecture:** Everything you work on—including your workflows, notes, databases, and chat histories—is now strictly tied to its respective project. This ensures your data remains completely organized and isolated.

### **💬 Streamlined Chat & Workspace Navigation**

Conversing with your knowledge base is smoother and more intuitive than ever.

- **Full History Embedded Chats:** Embedded chat interfaces have been updated into a full layout that displays your entire conversation history. Users can seamlessly click through and navigate across individual past chat sessions.
